Risk Prediction Dashboard

Project Summary

This project was during my time as a project manager in the Gies Disruption Lab. Disruption Lab allows students to explore interests in disruptive technologies by developing real-world solutions for clients. In previous semester, Disruption Lab engineers developed ML and NLP models that produced risk scores on the basis of 50+ data features. Our team built out a dashboard so our client could interact with the models.

As the project manager for this project, I led a team of 8 software engineers in the end-to-end productionization of a risk prediction dashboard for an auto-loan company

I clarified and communicated use cases between the client and engineers and provided oversight to the project as a whole. I wrote technical documents to onboard engineers onto the project and prepared midpoint and final demos for the client.
