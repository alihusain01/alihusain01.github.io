GPT Powered Flashcard Generator

Project Summary

I developed a web application that utilizes React and GPT-3 to automate the generation of flashcards from uploaded PowerPoint slides.

Users can upload their slides, and the application instantly generates comprehensive flashcards.

GPT-3 analyzes the slide content and creates concise and accurate flashcard prompts. This eliminates the need for manual flashcard creation, making studying and reviewing information more efficient.

The integration of React and GPT-3 provides a streamlined method for transforming slide content into interactive study materials, enhancing the learning experience.
